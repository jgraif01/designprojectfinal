The Best Executed Concept

Concepts can be difficult to execute, whether they’re mature, cute or cool, they can be very hard to style.

In most cases, like here, Tomorrow X Together’s Blue Orangeade, concepts are styled very well. The concept of the song “Blue Orangeade” is very fun and childish. Blue Orangeade is about complimenting each other’s personalities in a relationship. The vibe of the song is very upbeat and fun. Even the title of the song Blue Orangeade sounds like a sports drink flavour. The song and concept are very colourful and are full of fun tones.

For this stage, the boys are dressed in very vibrant colours. There is an array of neons, and hot colours. The outfits have sort of a childish vibe to them, matching the tone of the song. Every boy is wearing a belt, and they are wearing colours that compliment each other. There are even a few rainbows in their outfits.

In conclusion, their outfits match the vibe of the song very well and the concept of the song was very well executed.
