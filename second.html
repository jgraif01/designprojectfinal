The Best of K-pop Fashion

There are many executions of amazing outfits in K-pop. They always match the song, concept, and the members match each other. For today’s example, I will be breaking down Twice’s Cry For Me outfits and why they are some of the best in K-pop fashion.

There are 5 main elements in these costumes that make the girls match each other so well:
  • Specially detailed corsets/belts.
  • Ruffles.
  • Chokers.
  • Sheer fabric.
  • High heels.


All of these elements are used for the girls outfits more than once. Making them compliment each other properly. The same colour palette is used as well; white, silver and black. Everyone’s outfit works individually, and as a group. They all look absolutely stunning.

The concept for Cry For Me is what is called a “Mature” concept. Unlike Twice’s other cutesy and girl crush title tracks, Cry For Me has more of a sophisticated and grown up theme to it. Twice had been wanting to try this new concept for a while, and it is a really good concept for them. In a more mature concept, typically darker colours are imagined, liked grey, black, etc.

The outfits are extremely appealing to the eye, match well, and fit the concept. Therefore, making them perfect for this stage.
