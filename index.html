Design Project: The Best and Worst of K-pop Fashion

The Best and Worst of K-pop Fashion

By: Jacqueline Graif

Outfits are one of the most important elements of K-pop. The music genre is very famous for its stages. Outfits need to be made to match the concept of the song or music video and need to be comfortable enough to dance in. In my project, I will go over multiple outfits and rate them based on concept accuracy, comfortability and overall look.

Stages = live performance by a K-pop group of their song(s). Done specifically for Korean television.
